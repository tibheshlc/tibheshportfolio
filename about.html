About

Tibhesh Lamichhane

Photographer & Visual Artist — Kathmandu, Nepal

Your Portrait

images/tibhesh-self.jpg

I am a photographer and visual artist primarily working in commercial and fashion projects, collaborating with Nepal's biggest names. I have shot campaigns for Pepsi Nepal, 7Up Bangladesh, Sunsilk, and Alice Restaurant Group — leading creative vision, directing teams of stylists and designers, and overseeing post-production from start to finish.

Beyond commercial work, I travel across Nepal for personal documentary and editorial projects — trying to reveal the unseen narratives of life and humanity through my lens.
